This page is in progress. I am preparing a public repository that will provide the community with an end‑to‑end framework for performing milliarcsecond‑precision X‑ray–to-optical astrometry in strongly lensed quasars. The release will include:

- _Chandra_ data extraction scripts for relevant for high-precision astrometric analysis,
-  _Chandra_ ray-tracing modules for generating realistic simulated _Chandra_ observations at the same positions as the optical lensed images,
-  Tools for source-plane X-ray contour reconstruction  around the source-plane optical emission region for an array of putative solutions with pre-computed maximum likelihood values,
-  Demonstration notebooks showcasing the application of the above tools to highly-magnified lensed quasars with publicly accessible X-ray and optical datasets.

These tools form the backbone of my current research program on sub-kpc AGN structure and emission-region offsets. Please check back soon for the full release!
